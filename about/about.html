About

I am a senior at Carnegie Mellon University working towards a B.S. in Materials Science and Engineering with a double major in Biomedical Engineering.  I am interested in the application of materials engineering to the design and development of consumer products.  Currently, I am working with my BME capstone team to design a hydraulic cardiac sleeve to apply torsion to the hearts of patients with congestive heart failure.  I have experience working on projects with materials ranging from wood to bronze on nano and macro scale, as well as biointerfaces and medical devices.

Feel free to reach out with any questions or comments - rmccoy@andrew.cmu.edu.
